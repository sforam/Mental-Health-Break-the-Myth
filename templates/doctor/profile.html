I am a Psychiatrist, trained from National institute of mental health and Neurosciences (NIMHANS) and Kasturba medical college, Mangalore with more than 12 years of experience in this field. I was inspired to pursue a career in the field of Psychiatry whilst I was pursuing my medical internship. It ...provided me with an opportunity to work, build awareness and further understand problems related to the mind. As a student of medicine, I have always firmly believed in not just evaluating the symptoms, but understanding my client as a whole. I take a very individualistic approach while assessing my clients. My aim is to ensure that they have the ability to make informed decisions when it comes to their treatment plan. I’m aware of how challenging it is to live with a mental health concern. And so, I strive to guide them in their journey of recovery from mental illnesses, and help them achieve their personal mental health goals.
